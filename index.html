How To Use

  1. Use https://sub.htibinak.ccwu.cc/ip.txt when you need the raw priority feed file.
  2. Use cf.htibinak.ccwu.cc when you want the Cloudflare DNS endpoint maintained by cfnb.
  3. The first line in ip.txt is your fixed custom entry and stays pinned during refreshes.
  4. This page only publishes the latest generated feed. It does not convert the feed into Clash YAML.
